## Entity Status Determination

| Status | Qualification | Discount |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| **Micro Entity** | Gross income < $229,269/year AND ≤4 prior US patent applications | 75% off |
| **Small Entity** | < 500 employees | 50% off |
| **Large Entity** | Default | 0% |

> **Recommendation:** If you qualify as **Micro Entity**, file Form SB/15a (Certification) to save 75%.
> Most independent inventors qualify. This saves ~$10,000 across 3 patents.

## Cost Reduction Options

### Option A: Reduce Claims Count
- Patent 3 has 44 claims (14 independent) — the most expensive
- Patent 1 has 34 claims (10 independent), Patent 2 has 30 claims (10 independent)
- Reducing to 20 total / 3 independent saves significantly per patent
- **NOT RECOMMENDED** — the extra claims are critical for preventing design-arounds
- The broad generic claims (P1-C31, P2-C26, P3-C39) are the most valuable claims in the portfolio

### Option B: File as Provisional First ($350 × 3 = $1,050)
- Reserves priority date for 12 months
- Gives time to raise funds for non-provisional
- **Already discussed and rejected by inventor — going direct to non-provisional**

### Option C: PCT (International) After US Filing
- Cost: ~$4,000 additional per patent
- Extends protection to 150+ countries
- 30-month deadline from priority date
- Consider only if licensing to international companies
